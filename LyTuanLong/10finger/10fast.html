Interactive Keyboard

I was inspired to do this by looking around my desk. I thought it would be a fun idea to replicate the aesthetic and functionality of my Apple Wireless Keyboard.

To use this virtual keyboard, click on the text input below and begin typing on your physical keyboard. Watch as the keys are highlighted on the screen.
